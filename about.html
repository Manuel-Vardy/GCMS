About GCMS

A secure, government-managed digital platform designed to modernize the registration, verification, and management of Ordinance and Customary marriages across Ghana.

GCMS is a secure, government-managed digital platform designed to modernize the registration and management of marriages and divorces across Ghana. It provides a seamless, end-to-end solution that supports Ordinance and Customary marriages, ensuring legal compliance, accuracy, and efficiency.

Through the GCMS, citizens can submit applications online, upload required documents, pay statutory fees, track application status, and receive digitally signed marriage or divorce certificates.

GCMS strengthens Ghana’s civil registration system, enhances service delivery, promotes transparency, and provides reliable, verifiable records.

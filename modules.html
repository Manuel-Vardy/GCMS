System Architecture

Core Modules

A secure, government-managed digital platform designed to modernize the registration, verification, and management of Ordinance and Customary marriages across Ghana.

Core System Architecture

Registration Module

The core engine for capturing all necessary legal and personal data efficiently. This module ensures that all marriage records are started with precision and accuracy.

  • Marriage Registration Module: Digital capturing of marriage types (Civil, Customary, Islamic), dates, and officiating officers.
  • Spouses Information Module: Secure entry of personal identities, biometric data, and marital history for both parties.
  • Witness Information Module: Recording of legal witnesses, including digital signatures and identification verification.

Verification and Validation Module

Ensuring data integrity through strict identity checks and external database cross-referencing.

  • Identity Verification: Real-time integration with National Identity frameworks (e.g., Ghana Card) to validate citizen status and eligibility.

WorkStation Module

A hierarchical administrative environment supporting Ghana's governance structure across all levels.

  • Role-Based Workflow: Specific dashboards and permissions for:
    Registrar Supervisors Municipal District Metropolitan Regional National
  • Application Review & Approval: Multi-stage digital pipeline where applications are vetted, queried, and approved by relevant authorities.

Certificate Issuance Module

Automated generation of high-security marriage certificates. Each document features unique QR verification codes and digital seals, supporting both digital downloads and physical printing.

Divorce Module

Secure management of marital dissolution records. This module hooks into the central registry to update marital statuses instantly upon legal decree, ensuring the database remains a true source of state reality.

Connectivity & Intel

Integration Module (API)

Enterprise-grade API layer for cross-platform data synchronization.

  • Real-time sync with NIA
  • Births & Deaths Registry hooks
  • Third-party legal verification
Data Analytics Module

Turning registration data into actionable national intelligence.

  • Regional demographic reporting
  • National marriage trends
  • Resource allocation heatmaps

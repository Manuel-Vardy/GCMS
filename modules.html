System Architecture

Core Modules

Discover the interconnected systems that power GCMS. From initial application to final certificate issuance, these specialized components work in harmony to deliver a seamless, transparent, and secure civil registration experience for all citizens.

Core System Architecture

A secure, government-managed digital platform designed to modernize the registration, verification, and management of marriages across Ghana.

Registration Module

The core engine for capturing all necessary legal and personal data efficiently. This module ensures that all marriage records are started with precision and accuracy.

Marriage Registration Module

Digital capturing of marriage types (Civil, Customary, Islamic), dates, and officiating officers.

Spouses Information Module

Secure entry of personal identities, biometric data, and marital history for both parties.

Witness Information Module

Recording of legal witnesses, including digital signatures and identification verification.

Key Benefits
  • No manual queues or paper filing
  • Lower risk of errors and lost records
  • Faster issuance of certificates
  • Visibility for authorised government staff
  • Digital audit trail and accountability

Verification and Validation Module

After filing, the Ghana Centralized Marriage System waits a statutory period (normally 21 days).

During this time, authorised users can lodge objections (caveats) electronically. With GCMS, government staff can review objections and approve or reject before certificate issuance. The module also provide identity verification of all applicants by real-time integration with National Identity frameworks.

WorkStation Module

Ghana Centralized Marriage System has a Workstation Module that secures internal interface used by registrars, supervisors, and authorized government staff to process, review, approve, and manage marriage registration applications. It serves as the operational backbone of the Marriage Registration System.

  • Role-Based work flow: Efficient navigation and task assignment based on user responsibilities.
  • Application review & Approval: Secure multi-stage vetting process.

Certificate Issuance Module

The Certificate Issuance and Generation feature automates the creation, validation, and delivery of legally recognized marriage certificates from GCMS.

Divorce Module

The Divorce Module is a secure component in GCMS designed to manage the registration, validation, and documentation of divorce proceedings in accordance with Ghana's legal framework. It ensures that divorce records are properly processed, recorded, and linked to existing marriage records within the national civil registration system.

Integration Module (API)

The API Integration Component enables GCMS to securely communicate and exchange data with other authorized government systems and third-party platforms. It ensures interoperability, real-time verification, and seamless service delivery across Ghana's digital government ecosystem.

Data Analytics Module

The Data Analytics Module is a strategic component of GCMS that transforms registration data into actionable insights for government planning, monitoring, and policy development. The module collects and analyzes aggregated marriage and divorce data to generate real-time statistics and trend reports at municipal, district, regional, and national levels. It provides dashboards and visual reports on all indicators.

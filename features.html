System Capabilities

Key Features

A secure, government-managed digital platform designed to modernize the registration, verification, and management of Ordinance and Customary marriages across Ghana.

Certificate Generation

The Certificate Generation Module is a core component of GCMS responsible for the secure creation, validation, and issuance of official marriage and divorce certificates.

Once an application has successfully completed all statutory requirements such as verification, approval, and notice period compliance the system automatically generates a standardized government-approved certificate. Each certificate is assigned a unique identification number and is digitally signed by an authorized registrar to ensure authenticity. This module ensures accuracy, legal validity, and protection against fraud while improving the speed and efficiency of certificate issuance nationwide.

Security and Governance

The Security and Governance Module ensures that GCMS operates in a secured, accountable, and compliant manner, protecting sensitive citizen data and maintaining trust in Ghana’s civil registration system.

This feature protects citizen information, prevents fraud, ensures legal compliance, and promotes transparent, accountable management of marriage and divorce records.

Citizen-Facing

The Citizen-Facing Module is the public interface of the Marriage Registration Software, designed to make marriage and divorce registration accessible, transparent, and convenient for all Ghanaian citizens.

Accessibility compliance for Disabled

The Accessibility Compliance Module ensures that the Marriage Registration Software is inclusive and usable by all citizens, including persons with disabilities, in line with universal design principles and accessibility standards. This module ensures that the system provides equitable access to marriage and divorce registration services, promoting inclusion, independence, and equal opportunities for all citizens in Ghana.
